artificial intelligence of things aiot initiative at iit jodhpur aims to create facilities for design development prototyping and delivery of aiot systems for msmes and industries catering to applications of industry agriculture healthcare infrastructure energy environment and water management
two important facilities are created for the development of aiot technologies by faculty and students of iit jodhpur with participation of industry and other academic partners aiot fab facility the fab is equipped with set of instruments for the development characterization of sensors of various kinds that are the foundation of an iot ecosystem providing devices with the ability to collect data
the fab facility is undergoing further updates with the addition of new instruments
the aiot fab also focuses on the development of new sensing materials and targets applications based on photonics biotechnology and solar cells
it also envisions developing flexible sensors that can be used for making wearable devices along with other inertial sensors utilising mems technology
aiot electronic manufacturing unit emu the aiot emu facility is to be equipped with eda cad software for designing targeted sensors signal conditioning interface circuit fabless design pcb design
the emu facility will also house chip electronic component assembly along with hardware and software platforms that are required for developing ai applications
